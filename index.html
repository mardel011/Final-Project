Digitizing the Supply Chain on the Blockchain Platform

MAV CO.

MAV CO. specializes in facilitating supply chain traceability which creates value to stakeholders and empowers consumers to make informed purchasing decisions. With blockchain technology, MAV CO. can offer secure transactions and data information that is transparent, efficient, and scalable. We will work to customize a solution for your needs that will help track the movement of goods and payments across the various channels of the supply chain network.

Projects

MAV CO. is currently working with a palm oil consortium, which includes farmers, mill owners, manufacturers, suppliers, and retailers, to facilitate palm oil supply chain traceability. MAV CO. has partnered with MBI Tech to create a private and permissioned environment that will keep information viewable and/or editable by verified participants in the palm oil consortium.
